Equal height columns

Column one

CSS Flexbox makes it easy to get equal-height columns right out of the box.

Column two

Go ahead and resize this window.

The tallest flex-item on a row will determine the height of all its sibling flex-items on that row.

This happens on a per-row basis. If the flex-items wrap to multiple rows, then each row can have a different height, given by the tallest flex-item rendered on it.
